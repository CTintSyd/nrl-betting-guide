How to Read This

  • Implied probability = 1 ÷ odds. If odds are $2.00, the bookmaker implies a 50% chance.
  • Expected Value (EV) = does this bet make mathematical sense? Positive EV means you have an edge; negative EV means the bookmaker does.
  • The goal is to find bets where your estimated probability is higher than the implied probability — that's where value lives.
  • Warning: Most recreational bets are negative EV. That's fine — but know what you're signing up for.
